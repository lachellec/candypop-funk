What is her style of music?

Katy Perry’s genre is more of a pop/pop rock kind of music. She has a lot of upbeat songs to get you dancing and you just can’t help but to sing along. She has California Girls, Teenage Dreams, Waking up in Vegas, etc. But she also has some slow songs that gets you singing like you just got your heart broken. There’s Love me, Unconditional, Ghost, Thinking of you, etc. Those songs are the emotional songs that get into your feelings and I feel like that’s just her. Her music is another way to express herself and show everyone who she is and what she is feeling.
