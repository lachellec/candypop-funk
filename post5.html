Katy Perry is a great role model.

She can also be a great role model to a bunch of people. She wears whatever she wants because she likes and doesn’t care what everyone else thinks. She chooses to be confident in herself and to not be afraid to express who she is. She sings fireworks which is basically telling everyone to embrace who you are and to not let anyone judge you. To not let anyone talk you into being something you’re not and to stand up for yourself and let whatever is inside of you shine. To love yourself for who you are and to not change a thing because of how someone else feels, be yourself and be confident.
