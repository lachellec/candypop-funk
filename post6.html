Katy Perry sings from the heart

Katy Perry sings from the heart and you can hear it from her voice. You listen to the lyrics and you can’t help but feel the hurt or hatred or happiness. It all comes from her heart and it all brings you in. if its happiness you sing with a smile, if its hurt you sing as if your sad because her persona in the song comes out and you can’t help but to feel it. If its hatred you feel the pain and for that two minutes you hate whoever the song is supposed to be about. She brings everything out from the heart and shares it with the world.
